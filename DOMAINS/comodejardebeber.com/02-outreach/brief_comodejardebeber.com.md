# Campaign Brief: comodejardebeber.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** comodejardebeber.com
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Alcohol Recovery / Sober-Curious Movement
- **Target Market:** Digital alcohol recovery platforms, telehealth addiction services, sober-curious apps targeting Spanish speakers
- **Value Prop:** "Cómo Dejar de Beber" = "How to Stop Drinking" in Spanish. The exact "how to" question millions of Spanish speakers ask when they're ready to change their relationship with alcohol. The sober-curious movement and digital recovery space is booming — but Spanish-language resources are severely underserved. This domain is the SEO front door to that market.
