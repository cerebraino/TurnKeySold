## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Confident, no-nonsense, engineering-respecting. DevOps/SRE audiences are skeptical of fluff — lead with substance. The name "no fail" is bold; match it with data and comps.
- **Cultural note:** For DevOps/engineering buyers, emphasize the *precision* of the name. "No fail" is a bold claim — but backed by engineering rigor, it becomes a powerful promise. Reference specific reliability metrics (uptime SLAs, MTTR improvements) in follow-ups.
- **Avoid:** Overpromising on what the domain alone can do. Engineers know a domain isn't a silver bullet. Position it as a *brand asset* that reinforces their engineering credibility.
