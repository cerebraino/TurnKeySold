## Phase 1: Who Already Uses "Muy Guay"?

"Muy guay" is the most universal Spanish expression for "very cool / awesome" — particularly dominant in Spain but used across the Spanish-speaking world. Unlike trademarked phrases, it's a common expression that no single entity owns, making the domain valuable as a *brand acquisition* rather than a defensive purchase.

### Trademarks
No active trademark found for "Muy Guay" as a standalone brand in Spain (OEPM) or EU (EUIPO) — it's considered a common descriptive phrase, which is exactly why owning the .com is strategically valuable. It can't be trademarked by a competitor, but the domain CAN be owned.
