## paycarai.com — Companion Domain

**Same buyer pool as paycar.ai.** The .com version offers trust/SEO for companies that prefer .com over .ai.

**Valuation:** Similar value range ($6K–$18K). The .com extension adds trust but loses the .ai tech signal.

**Strategy:** Offer as a bundle — paycar.ai + paycarai.com. The AI startup gets the .ai; the enterprise acquirer uses the .com for redirect/main brand.
