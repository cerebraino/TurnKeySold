## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Confident, de-stigmatizing, expert. This is a health/education pitch — NOT adult entertainment. Talk like a health-tech professional who isn't afraid to say "sex" directly.
- **De-stigmatization is everything:** The name's boldness is the value. Frame it as: "We say what we do. No euphemisms. No shame. That's why people trust us."
- **Avoid:** Clinical/dry healthcare language ("sexual dysfunction," "treatment protocol"). Avoid adult-industry framing. The right messaging is: empowerment, expertise, directness.
- **Cultural note:** "Top Sex" may read differently across cultures. For US/UK/European markets, it's direct and empowering. For more conservative markets, position as a premium/elite access brand rather than mass-market.
