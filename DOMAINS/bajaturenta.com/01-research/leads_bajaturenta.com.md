## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Lower Your Rent" — Spanish real estate savings domain. Targets rent negotiation services, rental affordability platforms, tenant advocacy tech, and real estate marketplaces. "Baja tu renta" is an action phrase with strong consumer appeal — rent affordability is a top concern for Spanish-speaking renters in the US and Latin America. Good for a rent negotiation service, rental search platform, or consumer advocacy tool.

**Comparables:**
- LowerMyRent.com — English equivalent concept
- RentNegotiation services — growing category (e.g., RentCheck, LeaseLock)
- Zillow Rentals, Apartments.com — major rental platforms
- Spanish real estate domains trade at $500–$3K

**Brandability:** 36/50 Tier 2 — Clear action, consumer benefit. Three words but "baja tu renta" is a natural Spanish command phrase. Good for a service brand or campaign landing page.
