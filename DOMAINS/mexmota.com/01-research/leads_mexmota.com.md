## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Mexican Weed" — Spanish/English slang blend. "Mota" is the most common Mexican slang term for marijuana. This domain targets the legal cannabis industry with a specific Latino/Mexican cultural angle. Mexico is moving toward full legalization, and the US Hispanic cannabis market is growing rapidly. Good for a cannabis brand, dispensary chain, or lifestyle platform targeting the Mexican and Mexican-American market.

**Comparables:**
- Mota.com — premium, registered
- MexiCannabis.com — similar concept
- WeedMaps.com — $1.5B+ cannabis tech platform
- Spanish cannabis domains trade at $2K–$8K for brandables

**Brandability:** 35/50 Tier 2 — Short (7 letters), brandable, slang-authentic. Mexican cultural connection is a differentiator. "Mex" prefix + "mota" is catchy but niche.

**Value:** $500–$4,000 | **Anchor:** $3,000
