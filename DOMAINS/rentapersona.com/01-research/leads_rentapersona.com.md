## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Rent a Person" — Spanish gig economy domain, similar to rentahumanos.com but using "persona" (person) instead of "humanos" (humans). Slightly more personal, less industrial feel. Good for companion-style services, personal assistant platforms, companionship apps, elder care matching, or broader gig marketplace. "Persona" is warmer and more individual-focused than "humanos."

**Comparables:**
- RentAPerson.com — registered
- Care.com — $2B+ care marketplace (includes companionship)
- Cuideo.com — Spanish elder care platform
- Spanish gig platforms trade at $1K–$5K

**Brandability:** 36/50 Tier 2 — Warmer than "rentahumanos." Individual-focused, clear action. Three words but "renta persona" flows well in Spanish. Good for person-to-person service platforms.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $2,000
