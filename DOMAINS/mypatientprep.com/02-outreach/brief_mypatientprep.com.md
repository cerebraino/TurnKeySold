# Campaign Brief: mypatientprep.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** mypatientprep.com
- **Niche:** Healthcare SaaS / Patient Engagement / Medical Practice Software
- **Target Market:** US healthcare providers, medical SaaS companies, EHR platforms
- **Value Prop:** "My Patient Prep" — the domain that captures the entire patient preparation workflow. From pre-appointment instructions to procedure preparation, medication reminders to post-visit follow-ups, this domain describes exactly what it does: prepare patients for better healthcare experiences. The "My" prefix adds a personal touch that patients trust and providers value.

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Patient Workflow Brand. "My Patient Prep" describes a complete product category: software that prepares patients for appointments, procedures, and follow-ups. For healthcare SaaS companies, it's the exact-match domain for a growing category — patient engagement and preparation is a multi-billion dollar market.
- **Key Talking Points:** Exact-match for "patient prep" searches — high intent in healthcare. "My" prefix creates patient ownership and trust. Applicable across appointment prep, procedure prep, surgical prep, and follow-up care. Healthcare SaaS is booming — patient engagement tools are in high demand. Three words that describe a complete product category.
- **Use cases:** Standalone patient engagement product, patient portal brand, appointment preparation app, or surgical prep platform.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Professional, patient-centered, solution-oriented. Healthcare SaaS buyers are sophisticated — focus on the product category and market opportunity.
- **Key insight:** "My Patient Prep" has a warmth that clinical healthcare brands often lack. The "My" prefix makes it personal — it's the patient's prep, not the hospital's process. That patient-centric framing is exactly where healthcare is heading.

## Objection Handling
- **"We already have a patient engagement product name":** "mypatientprep.com captures search traffic your branded product name can't. It's demand capture — when providers search for 'patient prep software' or patients search for 'my patient prep,' this domain brings them to you."
- **"'Patient prep' sounds narrow — we do more than that":** "Patient prep is the gateway to the entire patient journey. It encompasses scheduling, instructions, reminders, intake, follow-up — the full workflow. The domain is specific enough to be memorable and broad enough to cover a complete product suite."
- **"Three words is long for a domain":** "In healthcare SaaS, descriptive domains outperform branded ones for trust and conversion. 'My Patient Prep' tells the complete story — and 'MyChart' proved that 'My' + healthcare word is a winning formula."
