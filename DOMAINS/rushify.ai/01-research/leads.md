## 5. Outreach Recommendations

- **Top priority — DoorDash (Tony Xu):** The CEO who transformed food delivery into logistics. "Rushify" perfectly describes DoorDash's expansion beyond restaurant delivery.
- **Angle for DoorDash/Uber:** "You've moved from food delivery to everything delivery. rushify.ai is the brand that says 'we deliver anything, instantly.' It's the name for your next decade of growth."
- **Angle for Gopuff/Getir:** "Your entire business is 'rush.' That's the brand promise. rushify.ai captures it in one word — and tells customers you're AI-powered."
- **Open:** $25,000 | **Floor:** $10,000
- **Tone:** Energetic, forward-looking, ambitious. On-demand delivery is a winner-take-most market — strong brands matter.
- **Cultural note:** "Rushify" has broad cross-cultural appeal — "rush" is understood globally in logistics contexts, and "-ify" is a universal verb suffix in English-speaking markets.
- **Alternative pitch for B2B logistics (Stuart/Fabric):** Offer rushify.ai as a customer-facing brand for their B2B clients to use. Logistics platforms could power the "Rushify" app as their consumer brand.
