- **Domain:** fieldlogbook.com

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Digital Log Book. Every field crew knows what a log book is — it's the paper record of jobs done, hours worked, materials used. "Field Log Book" modernizes that concept: the digital, cloud-connected version. For an FSM software company, this domain can be a product name, a feature module, or an SEO landing page.
- **Key Talking Points:** "Log book" is a universally understood field service concept. Descriptive — tells field crews exactly what it does. Companion to fieldworkorder.com (dispatch + documentation). Growing FSM market — $5B+ and expanding.
