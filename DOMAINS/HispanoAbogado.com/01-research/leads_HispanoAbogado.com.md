### Suggested Angles (from campaign brief)
- **For Law Firms (Morgan & Morgan):** "You already spend millions on Spanish-language advertising. HispanoAbogado.com is the landing page that converts those dollars into clients more efficiently than any generic domain."
- **For Legal Directories:** "HispanoAbogado.com is a lead generation machine waiting to happen. 2M+ monthly searches for 'abogado' in the US alone. This domain pays for itself in PPC savings."
- **For Immigration Firms:** "When Spanish-speaking clients search for legal help, they search in Spanish. HispanoAbogado.com signals immediately, 'We speak your language — literally.'"

### Talking Points for Outreach Architect
- **Hot leads:** Morgan & Morgan (#1), FindLaw/Avvo (#8–9) — the legal marketing platforms may close faster than law firms
- **Tone (from brief):** Respectful, authoritative, community-focused. Emphasize how the domain removes barriers to legal access.
- **Cultural note:** For Hispanic-focused firms, the *trust* implied by a Spanish-language domain is the highest value proposition. Spanish-speaking clients are more likely to trust "HispanoAbogado.com" than "JohnDoeLaw.com."
- **Price anchoring:** Open at $22,000; floor at $8,000.
- **Scarcity:** "Abogado" is among the most expensive Spanish keywords in AdWords ($50–$75 CPC in some markets). The domain value justifies itself in ad savings alone.
- **SEO angle:** "HispanoAbogado.com is an exact-match SEO machine for 'abogado hispano' searches. Market the domain and it ranks organically."
- **Objection prep (from brief):** "We already have a Spanish page" → "A dedicated brand like HispanoAbogado sees 2–3× higher CTR because it signals a 100% committed Spanish experience."
