## 0. Strategic Foundations

### Brand
- **Name:** TurnKeySold Domains (a TurnKeySold venture)
- **Domain:** TurnKeySold.com
- **Tagline:** *Premium domains, sold on purpose.*
- **Positioning:** A turnkey domain sales service for premium domain owners who'd rather sell than wait.

### Audience
- **Primary:** Domain investors and portfolio owners with premium domains ($5k+), who lack time or system to find qualified buyers.
- **Secondary:** Individual domain owners with a high-value asset who want a professional sales campaign.

### The Core Differentiator (the wedge)
**Proactive vs. Passive.**
Marketplaces (Sedo, Afternic, GoDaddy) make you list and wait. We go find the buyer. That's the entire pitch.
> *"Stop waiting for a buyer. We go find them."*

### Brand Voice
Confident, professional, investor-grade. Not hypey. Not "used car lot." Think boutique investment firm. Transparent, results-oriented, trustworthy.

## 1. The Trust Architecture (most important section)

A new broker with no track record must over-index on trust. This is not a section — it's an architecture that runs through everything.

### 1.1 How You Get Paid (make it visible and prominent)
This defuses the #1 unspoken fear. Add a prominent block near the CTA and in the FAQ:

> **How you get paid, safely:** All transactions run through a licensed escrow service (e.g., Escrow.com). Funds are verified before any domain transfer. You never release your domain until payment is secured. We never touch your funds.

> *"You grant us authorization to market your domain — not ownership. You keep it in your registrar account the entire time. We bring you offers; you approve the sale; the transfer happens through escrow at closing."*

## 2. The AI Question **[DECISION]**

The enrichment doc flags this as a strategic fork. Our team is agentic (AI agents doing research and outreach). The target audience includes domain investors who own .ai domains themselves.

**Recommendation: Imply-leaning-Disclose**
> *"AI-powered buyer intelligence, human-reviewed outreach."*

This positions AI as a capability (smarter research, faster targeting) with a human-in-the-loop guarantee (every message is reviewed before sending). It's modern, honest, and a credibility asset with this audience — as long as we never let outreach read as bot-blasted.

**Impact on copy:**
- Hero can reference "AI-augmented buyer research"
- How It Works can mention "data-driven targeting powered by AI"
- Must pair with: "Every message is reviewed and approved by a human before it reaches a prospect"

**[DECISION: Owner — agree with "imply-leaning-disclose"? Want a different lane?]**

## 3. The DMPS Question **[DECISION]**

The enrichment doc asks: **Make it real, or cut it?** We actually have a real scoring system (DomainMatch Pro Score — 4 dimensions, 0–100, 5 tiers) created by our researcher. We can:

- **Make it real** — define the 4 inputs (Domain Fit, Prospect Quality, Company Fit, Purchase Urgency) as a transparent rubric. This is a credibility asset.
- **Soften it** — describe the research rigor in plain language without the acronym.

**Recommendation:** Make it real. A transparent scoring system signals sophistication and rigor. But don't over-hype it on the site — mention it in the methodology section as "our proprietary lead scoring system" with 2–3 inputs named, without dumping the full formula.

**[DECISION: Owner — keep DMPS as a real methodology mention, or drop the acronym?]**

## 4. Pricing & Offer Structure

### Success Fee
- **20%** of final sale price (standard)
- Low end (under $10K): consider 25%
- High end (over $50K): consider 15%
- *Not necessarily shown publicly; fee scales with sale size.*

### The Retainer Fix (critical contradiction fix)
The original brief said "No upfront fees" then listed a $500 retainer. This is a direct contradiction. Fix:

> *"You pay nothing unless we sell. Optionally, a $500 priority fee fast-tracks your campaign — fully credited against your success fee at closing."*

Making it **creditable** removes the friction.

### Minimum Domain Value
State the $5K floor on the pricing page, not just the FAQ:
> *"We work with premium domains typically valued at $5,000 or more. Not sure? Send it anyway — we'll tell you honestly."*

### 5B. HOW IT WORKS (Detailed Page)

**Headline:** The TurnKeySold Process

**Subheadline:** A systematic, research-driven approach. No guesswork. No spam. Just results.

**The Four Phases:**

**Phase 1: Assessment & Strategy** *(2–3 days)*
- Domain valuation using comparable sales data, brandability scoring, market analysis
- Industry and buyer persona mapping
- Pricing strategy: target, reserve, negotiation anchors
- **Deliverable:** Campaign Strategy Document

**Phase 2: Buyer Identification** *(3–5 days)*
- 10–15 hand-researched, qualified buyer prospects
- Decision-maker research: names, titles, verified contact information
- Lead scoring using our proprietary 4-dimension qualification system
- Intent signal detection: funding rounds, product launches, competitive moves
- **Deliverable:** Qualified Lead List

**Phase 3: Outreach Campaign** *(3–4 weeks)*
- Multi-channel sequence (email-first, LinkedIn follow-up)
- Personalized messaging for each buyer persona — AI-augmented intelligence, human-approved send
- 6-touch campaign structure over 18–21 days
- Real-time response handling and objection management
- Weekly progress reports
- **Deliverable:** Campaign Report with engagement metrics

**Phase 4: Negotiation & Closing** *(Variable)*
- Facilitate buyer-seller conversations
- Handle price negotiations within your approved range
- All payments through licensed escrow (Escrow.com)
- Coordinate domain transfer after funds are secured
- **Deliverable:** Closed Sale at Your Terms
