## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Action Brand. "Parar de Beber" is a command — not a question, not a suggestion. It's more brandable than the "how to" format and carries a decisive, empowering energy. For a sobriety app or recovery brand, this domain is the Spanish-language call to action.
- **Key Talking Points:** Direct action command — stronger than "how to" format. More brandable — could be a program or app name. Companion to comodejardebeber.com (content/SEO) covering both search intents. 14 characters — shorter than the "how to" version.
