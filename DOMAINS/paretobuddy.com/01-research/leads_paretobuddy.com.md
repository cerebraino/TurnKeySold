### Brandability Assessment
| Attribute | Score (1–10) | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Pronounceability | 7 | "Pa-reh-toh Bud-dy" — Pareto has a slight intellectual ring; non-native speakers may pause. Buddy is universal. |
| Spellability | 6 | "Pareto" is a proper name — not universally known how to spell. Could be confused with "Parito" or "Paretta." "Buddy" is easy. |
| Memorability | 8 | The 80/20 rule reference is clever and sticky for productivity enthusiasts. The "buddy" suffix makes it friendly. |
| Visual Aesthetic | 7 | Two words merged (11 chars + .com). Clean but longer. No hyphens/numbers. |
| Category Fit | 9 | Perfect for productivity tools. "Pareto" signals efficiency focus + "Buddy" signals friendly AI assistant. Great for the productivity SaaS category. |
| **Total** | **37/50 — Tier 2: Good Brandable** |
