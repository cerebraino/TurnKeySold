## 2. Trademark Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Dalai Lama association** | The word "Dalai" is inseparable from the Dalai Lama in most people's minds. Using it commercially creates brand confusion risk. |
| **Trademark status** | The Dalai Lama name is protected. However, "Dalai" alone may not be trademarked — it depends on use context and jurisdiction. |
| **Viable use cases** | Must be clearly unrelated to Tibetan Buddhism. Wellness/tech brands using "Dalai" as a coined word (not referring to the spiritual leader) may have a defensible position. |
| **Recommendation** | Only market to buyers who understand the risk and can demonstrate independent, non-confusing use. Not suitable for any brand that implies spiritual/Dalai Lama association. |

## 4. Outreach Recommendations

- **Emphasize "Dalai" as a coined word** — rhythmic, calming, 5-letter brand
- **Never reference** the Dalai Lama or Tibetan Buddhism in outreach
- **Target wellness/tech companies** who could frame "Dalai" as a fresh, invented brand name
- **Best use cases:** A meditation app wanting a short, rhythmic brand; a fitness platform wanting a premium .fit extension

### Bundle Strategy
