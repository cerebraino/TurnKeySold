## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Value Market. GLP-1 medications are expensive. "Cheap" is the most powerful price signal — it tells budget-conscious consumers "we have the affordable option you're looking for."
- **Key Talking Points:**
  - "Cheap" is a high-volume search term — millions search for "cheap weight loss meds" every month.
  - The GLP-1 affordability crisis is creating a massive budget segment.
  - "Cheap" doesn't mean low quality — it means accessible. Frame it as democratizing weight loss.
  - Complements the other Meds domains for companies that want coverage across all price tiers.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Direct, value-focused. GLP-1 affordability is a pressing issue — frame the domain as a solution for price-sensitive consumers.
- **Sensitive note:** "Cheap" can have negative connotations in healthcare. Position it as "affordable access" and "democratizing weight loss" rather than just "cheap."
- **Bundle note:** For Ro and Hims, lead with the full Meds family portfolio.
