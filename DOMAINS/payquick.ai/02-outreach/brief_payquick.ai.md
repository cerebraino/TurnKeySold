# Campaign Brief: payquick.ai

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** payquick.ai
- **Niche:** Fast Checkout / One-Click Payments / Express Fintech
- **Target Market:** Global — Payment processors, e-commerce platforms, one-click checkout startups
- **Value Prop:** "Pay Quick" = the universal promise of fast checkout. Speed is the new currency in payments — and this domain owns that promise.

## Target Buyer Personas
1. **The Payment Infrastructure Leader (Persona A):** CEOs of payment platforms (Stripe, Shopify Payments) where fast checkout is core to their product — "payquick" brands the speed of their checkout experience.
2. **The One-Click Checkout Startup (Persona B):** Fast-checkout disruptors (Bolt, Fast) that need a brand matching their ambition.
3. **The BNPL/Checkout Platform (Persona A/B):** Buy-now-pay-later platforms (Klarna, Afterpay) expanding into faster checkout.

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** Your Value Prop as a URL. "Pay Quick" is the promise every checkout platform makes. Owning this domain means your URL says it before you do.
- **Key Talking Points:**
  - Speed is the #1 factor in checkout conversion — "payquick" promises it.
  - Perfect for AI-powered checkout optimization (the .ai signals intelligence).
  - Short (8 chars + .ai), modern, and universally understood.
