# Campaign Brief: paguesucasa.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** paguesucasa.com
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Mortgage / Real Estate Payments — Formal Conjugation
- **Target Market:** Mortgage lenders, real estate fintech, home loan platforms targeting Spanish-speaking homebuyers
- **Value Prop:** "Pague Su Casa" = "Pay Your House" — the formal "usted" conjugation for mortgage and real estate payments in Spanish. Part of the expanding Spanish RE payment domain family alongside pagacasa.com, pagatucasa.com, and paguecasa.com. The "su" (formal "your") adds a professional, financial-services tone appropriate for mortgage lending.
