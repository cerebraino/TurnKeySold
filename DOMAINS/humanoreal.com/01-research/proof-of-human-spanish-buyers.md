The Spanish-speaking world (500M+ people) needs its own vocabulary and digital infrastructure for this question.

Two domains capture the entire Spanish-language proof-of-human spectrum:

- **humanoReal.com** = "Real Human" — the verification seal. "This account is humano real." Perfect for platforms, social networks, and identity companies.
- **PruebaDeHumanidad.com** = "Proof of Humanity" / "Humanity Test" — the process. The exact translation of the global Proof of Humanity movement. Could be THE Spanish-language hub for the entire PoH ecosystem.
