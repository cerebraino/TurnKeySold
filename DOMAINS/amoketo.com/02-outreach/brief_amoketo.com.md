## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Warm, passionate, community-focused. Keto has a devoted following — speak to that passion.
- **Cultural note:** "Amo" is a powerful, emotional word in Spanish. Frame the domain as a love letter to the keto community.
- **For US Hispanic market:** The keto diet is popular in Latin American cuisine (low-carb alternatives to rice, beans, tortillas). This is a cultural fit, not just a language translation.
- **Best channels:** Instagram, TikTok, YouTube — keto content thrives on visual platforms. The brand could be a content-first keto community.

## Objection Handling
- **"Keto is a fad — the market might shrink":** "Keto has been growing for 10+ years and is now a $15B+ market. Even if 'keto' as a term evolves, 'low-carb' and 'healthy lifestyle' aren't going anywhere. And amoketo.com's emotional resonance ('love') transcends any specific diet."
