# Campaign Brief: Puttey.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** Puttey.com — "Putt-ey" / "Putty" golf brand
- **Niche:** Golf Accessories / Putting Tech / Training Aids / Golf Apparel
- **Value Prop:** "Puttey" — a playful, memorable golf brand name. Works as both "putt-ey" (putting-related) and "putty" (malleable, shapeable). 6 letters, .com, and instantly associated with golf.

## Lead Research Summary
*Source: task description (lead, Jul 22)*

Top leads: Golf training aid brands, putting apps, golf accessory companies looking for a distinctive brand name.
