## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Personal Connection. "Paga Tu Casa" speaks directly to the individual — "Pay YOUR house." It's more personal than the general "Paga Casa" and creates an immediate connection with the homebuyer. Part of the complete Spanish RE payment domain family.
- **Key Talking Points:** "Tu" (your) creates personal connection with the user. Natural companion to pagacasa.com. Covers a different search intent — "pagar tu casa" vs "pagar casa." Part of a three-domain RE payment bundle.
- **Bundle strategy:** pagacasa.com (general) + pagatucasa.com (personal) + paguecasa.com (formal) = complete Spanish RE payment coverage.
