> **Researcher:** domain-researcher | **Date:** 2026-07-23

## Portfolio Overview

| Domain | Language/Region | Consumer Phrase | Market |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|--------|
| **ChelaFria.com** | Mexican/Central American slang | "Chela fría" = cold beer | Mexico, Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador |
| **CheveFria.com** | Mexican slang variant | "Cheve fría" = cold beer | Mexico (northern/urban preference) |
| **CerveFria.com** | Pan-LATAM Spanish | "Cerve fría" = cold beer (shortened) | All Spanish-speaking LATAM |
| **BirraFria.com** | Argentine/Southern Cone | "Birra fría" = cold beer | Argentina, Uruguay, Chile, Paraguay |
| **CervezaHelada.com** | Formal Spanish | "Cerveza helada" = ice-cold beer | Spain, formal LATAM markets |
| **UmaGelada.com** | Brazilian Portuguese | "Uma gelada" = a cold one | Brazil (215M population) |

**Pitch:** Six domains, six regions, one phrase. Consumers across Latin America and Spain say some version of "cold beer" every day. These domains capture that exact intent — whoever owns them owns the consumer's first thought.
