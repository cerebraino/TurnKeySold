- **Primary Angle:** The Betting App AI Domain. "App AI" on .bet — it's the most direct description possible: an AI app for betting. While aiapp.bet reverses the word order ("AI App"), appai.bet puts "App" first — capturing a slightly different search intent. Together with appaibet.com, they form the complete AI betting app domain family.
- **Key Talking Points:** Perfect TLD match — "App AI" on .bet. Short domain — 6 characters (appai). Companion to aiapp.bet — different word order, same concept. Bundle with appaibet.com for .com coverage. Growing .bet TLD adoption.
