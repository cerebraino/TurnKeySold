# Campaign Brief: rentapersona.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** rentapersona.com
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Person-to-Person Services / Care Marketplace
- **Target Market:** Care platforms, personal service marketplaces, companionship apps targeting Spanish-speaking markets
- **Value Prop:** "Renta Persona" = "Rent a Person" in Spanish. Warmer and more personal than "renta humanos" — "persona" implies an individual connection. Perfect for care services, elder companionship, tutoring, or personal assistance platforms. The companion to rentahumanos.com — together they cover the bold and the personal angles of the gig economy.

- **Primary Angle:** The Personal Connection Brand. Where "renta humanos" is bold and industrial, "renta persona" is warm and individual. It positions the service as person-to-person connection — ideal for care, tutoring, companionship, and personal assistance. Together with rentahumanos.com, the pair covers the full gig economy spectrum.
- **Key Talking Points:** "Persona" = warmer, more individual than "humanos." Ideal for care, tutoring, and personal services. Flows naturally in Spanish — "renta persona" is a natural phrase. Companion to rentahumanos.com — bold + personal.
