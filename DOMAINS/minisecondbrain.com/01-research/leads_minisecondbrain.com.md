## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Mini Second Brain" — lightweight personal knowledge management. The "Second Brain" methodology was popularized by Tiago Forte (fortelabs.com, bestselling book *Building a Second Brain*). This domain targets the PKM (Personal Knowledge Management) ecosystem: note-taking apps, AI-powered knowledge tools, and productivity platforms. "Mini" positions it as a simpler, more accessible entry point — ideal for a consumer app, browser extension, or lightweight companion product.

**Comparables:**
- SecondBrain.com — registered, not publicly sold
- Notion.so — $10B valuation company, dominates PKM space
- Obsidian.md — $10M+ ARR, "second brain" in marketing
- Reflect.app — uses "second brain" positioning

**Brandability:** 40/50 Tier 2 — "Mini Second Brain." Three words but the "second brain" phrase is a recognized category term. Memorable, clearly communicates value proposition. Good for a consumer SaaS product.

**Value:** $2,000–$8,000 | **Anchor:** $6,000
