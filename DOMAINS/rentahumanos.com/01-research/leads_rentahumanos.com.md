## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Rent Humans" — Spanish gig economy/staffing domain. Targets on-demand labor platforms, temporary staffing, freelance marketplaces, or "human-as-a-service" platforms serving the Spanish-speaking market. Could also work for a satirical or commentary site about gig economy exploitation. The phrasing is provocative — good for a brand that wants to stand out.

**Comparables:**
- RentAHuman.com — registered, similar concept
- TaskRabbit.com — gig platform ($100M+ acquired by IKEA)
- Workana.com — LatAm freelance marketplace ($10M+ funded)
- Spanish gig economy domains trade at $1K–$5K

**Brandability:** 34/50 Tier 2 — Provocative, memorable, clear value prop. Three words but punchy. The "rent" framing is edgy but eye-catching. Good for a disruptive gig platform.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $2,000
