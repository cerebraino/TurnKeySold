- **Domain:** acoholfree.com
- **Niche:** Alcohol-Free Beverages / Sober-Curious Movement / Wellness

- **Primary Angle:** The Category Domain. The non-alcoholic beverage market is one of the fastest-growing consumer categories. "Alcohol Free" is the phrase everyone uses to describe it. This domain captures the category at the exact moment consumers are searching for alternatives.
- **Key Talking Points:** Exact-match for "alcohol free" — high commercial intent search. Sober-curious movement is booming — Gen Z drinking 20% less than previous generations. Can serve as a directory, ecommerce platform, or redirect. The "a" prefix is a minor quirk that doesn't affect recognition.
