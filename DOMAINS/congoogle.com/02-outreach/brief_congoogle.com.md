# Campaign Brief: congoogle.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** congoogle.com
- **Niche:** ⚠️ TRADEMARK-RESTRICTED — Google-related Spanish domain
- **Target Market:** **Extremely limited** — only Google itself can legally use this domain
- **Value Prop:** "Con Google" = "With Google" in Spanish. A descriptive Spanish phrase domain. However, it incorporates the GOOGLE trademark, which severely limits its market. This brief is for reference only — most domain marketplaces will not list trademark-infringing domains.

## Lead Research Summary
*Source: leads_congoogle.com.md (domain-researcher, Jul 22)*

> ⚠️ **CRITICAL TRADEMARK NOTE:** "Google" is a registered trademark of Google LLC. This domain's legal buyer pool is essentially limited to Google itself. Third-party use would constitute trademark infringement. Most domain marketplaces (Sedo, Afternic, SquadHelp) will refuse to list domains containing "Google."

**Potential buyers (limited to Google entities):**
1. **Google LatAm** (DMPS 80) — LatAm VP. Spanish-language Google operations.
2. **Google Cloud Spanish** (DMPS 75) — LatAm Director. Cloud services for Spanish-speaking markets.
