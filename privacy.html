When you submit a domain valuation request through our website, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number (if provided), domain name, and target price (if provided). We also collect basic analytics data about page views and interactions on our website.

We use the information you provide to evaluate your domain submission, communicate with you about our services, and provide a confidential valuation and campaign proposal. We use analytics data to improve our website and understand how visitors interact with our content.
