## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Cancel Subscriptions" — Spanish consumer fintech domain. Targets the subscription management space: apps that help users track, manage, and cancel recurring subscriptions. Rocket Money (formerly Truebill) proved this is a massive category ($1B+ acquisition by Rocket Companies). Spanish-language market is underserved — 500M+ Spanish speakers worldwide with growing digital subscription adoption.

**Comparables:**
- CancelSubscriptions.com — English equivalent
- RocketMoney.com — $1.275B acquisition (Rock Companies, 2021)
- BobbyApp.com — subscription tracker
- Spanish fintech domains trade at $1K–$5K

**Brandability:** 38/50 Tier 2 — Clear action verb, high-intent keyword. Long (23 chars) but perfectly descriptive for SEO. "Cancelar suscripciones" is exactly what users search for. Good for a Spanish-market app or a Spanish-language landing page for an existing product.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $2,500
