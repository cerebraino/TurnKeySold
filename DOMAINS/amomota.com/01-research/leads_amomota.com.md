## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "I Love Weed" — Spanish cannabis brand domain. "Amo mota" is a catchy, emotionally positive cannabis phrase — "mota" is the most widely used Mexican/Spanish slang for marijuana. Good for a cannabis lifestyle brand, dispensary chain, CBD product line, cannabis media platform, or cannabis-friendly social network targeting the Spanish-speaking market. The "love" framing is upbeat and brand-friendly.

**Comparables:**
- ILoveWeed.com — English equivalent
- AmoCannabis.com — similar concept, more formal
- Mota.com — premium cannabis domain
- Spanish cannabis brand domains trade at $1K–$5K

**Brandability:** 39/50 Tier 2 — Short (7 letters), emotionally positive, brandable. "Amo mota" has rhythm and authenticity. Excellent for a consumer-facing cannabis brand. The slang feel gives it street credibility while remaining approachable.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $2,000
