# Campaign Brief: humanoreal.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** humanoreal.com
- **Niche:** AI Verification / Human Authentication / Proof of Personhood
- **Target Market:** Identity verification platforms, AI detection tools, "proof of humanity" technology companies
- **Value Prop:** "Humano Real" = "Real Human" in Spanish. As AI-generated content, deepfakes, and bots proliferate, proving you're a "real human" is becoming one of tech's most valuable problems to solve. This domain captures that verification concept in Spanish — offering differentiation in a space dominated by English-language brands. For identity verification platforms or AI detection companies, it's a Spanish-language brand for the "real human" verification category.
