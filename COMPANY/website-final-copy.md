#### HOW IT WORKS

**Section headline:**
> From Listed to Sold. Here's How.

**Step 0 — Fit Check**
> Not every domain qualifies — and that's the point. We review every submission against our internal criteria. If we believe we can sell it, we'll tell you. If not, we'll tell you that too.

**Step 1 — Research & Target**
> We analyze your domain through comparable sales data, brandability scoring, and industry fit. Then we identify 10–15 hand-researched, qualified buyers — companies, startups, or individuals who would genuinely benefit from owning your domain.

**Step 2 — Personalized Outreach**
> We craft a custom multi-channel campaign for each prospect. Our outreach uses AI-augmented buyer intelligence for precision targeting, and every message is reviewed and approved by a human before it reaches anyone. Each touchpoint sounds like one professional talking to another — not a blast.

**Step 3 — Negotiate & Close**
> We handle the conversations, objections, and negotiations. All payments run through licensed escrow (e.g., Escrow.com). Funds are verified before any domain transfer. We never touch your money. You approve the terms. You get paid.

> A: Premium .com, .ai, .io, and select country TLDs typically valued at $5,000+. We review each submission individually.

## 6. Design & Technical Notes for the Builder

| Element | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Design style** | Clean, minimal, premium. Generous white space. Strong typography. |
| **Font** | Sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or similar) |
| **Color palette** | Neutral/white background. Deep navy or charcoal for headers. A single accent color (gold, amber, or deep green) for CTAs and highlights. |
| **Layout** | Full-width hero. Card-based sections (portfolio, trust cards). Single-column on mobile. |
| **Mobile** | All sections must stack vertically on mobile. Portfolio cards degrade to single column. CTAs must be tappable. |
| **Speed** | Optimize images. Minimal JavaScript. Fast load time. |
| **SEO** | Every page needs: meta title, meta description, OG image, Open Graph tags. |
| **Analytics** | Plausible, Fathom, or Google Analytics (choose one). |
| **Forms** | Contact form should send to hello@turnkeysold.com. Use Formspree, Web3Forms, or serverless function. |
| **Hosting** | Deploy on Vercel (or Netlify). |
| **Footer** | Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages needed (placeholder text acceptable at launch). |
