# Campaign Brief: fightrenthike.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** fightrenthike.com
- **Niche:** Tenant Advocacy / Rent Control / Housing Rights
- **Target Market:** Tenant unions, legal aid organizations, housing rights nonprofits, proptech advocacy platforms
- **Value Prop:** "Fight Rent Hike" — a complete call to action in three words. As rental affordability becomes a crisis in major cities, tenant advocacy is growing into a significant movement. This domain is the rallying point: a platform for organizing, legal resources, rent control campaigns, and tenant education. Action-oriented, emotionally resonant, and immediately clear in its mission.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Urgent, action-oriented, mission-driven. This is advocacy — the domain should feel like a call to action.
- **Key insight:** "Fight Rent Hike" is politically charged — and that's the point. Advocacy organizations need domains that inspire action, not just describe services.

## Objection Handling
- **"'Fight' is too aggressive for our organization's tone":** "Advocacy requires clarity. 'Fight' signals that you're on the tenant's side — not neutral, not negotiating, but fighting. That clarity builds trust and drives engagement with the people who need you most."
- **"Three words is long for a domain":** "It's not just a domain — it's a slogan. 'Fight Rent Hike' is what you'd put on a protest sign, a campaign flyer, or a social media post. The domain is the campaign."
