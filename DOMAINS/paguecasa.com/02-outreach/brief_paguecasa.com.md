# Campaign Brief: paguecasa.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** paguecasa.com
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Real Estate Payments — Formal Conjugation
- **Target Market:** Latin America, Spain, US Hispanic — same buyer pool as pagacasa.com
- **Value Prop:** "Pague Casa" = "Pay House" (formal/usted conjugation). The third conjugation in the Spanish RE payment domain family. "Pague" is the formal "usted" form — used in professional, business, and official contexts. Together with pagacasa.com (informal command) and pagatucasa.com (personal "tú"), this trio covers every way Spanish speakers search for home payments.
