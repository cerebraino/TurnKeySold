## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Take Your House" — Spanish real estate domain, can be read two ways: (1) "quíte" as in "remove/take away" — eviction defense or mortgage relief, or (2) "quite" as in "take possession" — home buying/ownership transfer. The ambiguity gives it range across real estate use cases. Targets real estate platforms, legal services, mortgage companies, and housing advocacy orgs.

**Comparables:**
- TakeYourHouse.com — English equivalent
- QuitaTuCasa.com — informal variant
- SuCasa.com — premium Spanish real estate domain
- Spanish real estate domains trade at $500–$3K

**Brandability:** 33/50 Tier 2 — Action-oriented, dual meaning gives flexibility. Three words. The formal "su" form sounds professional. Less clear brand than bajaturenta.com but covers a broader real estate range.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $1,500
