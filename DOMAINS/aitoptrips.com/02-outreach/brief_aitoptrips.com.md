## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Complementary, not competitive. Always position aitoptrips.com as the planning half of the travel pair — never pitch it against aitoptravel.com.
- **Key insight:** "Trips" is a warmer, more personal word than "travel." "Travel" is the industry; "trips" are what people actually take. That personal connection makes aitoptrips.com especially strong for consumer-facing AI trip planning tools.
