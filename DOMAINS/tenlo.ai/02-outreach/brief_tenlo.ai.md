# Campaign Brief: tenlo.ai

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** tenlo.ai
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Commerce / On-Demand Delivery / E-Commerce AI
- **Target Market:** Latin America, Spain, US Hispanic — 500M+ Spanish speakers
- **Value Prop:** "Tenlo" = "Have it." A short, punchy Spanish command verb — exactly what you say when you want something delivered, downloaded, or acquired. Combined with .ai, it's the domain for AI-powered commerce, instant delivery, or recommendation platforms serving the Spanish-speaking world. Five characters. One command. Infinite possibility.
