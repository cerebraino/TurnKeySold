## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Field Log Book" — mobile workforce documentation. Targets field service management (FSM) software companies, construction tech, oil & gas inspection, utility field crews, and any industry where workers log activities in the field. Clear enterprise SaaS positioning. The term "log book" is universally understood in field operations.

**Comparables:**
- FieldLog.com — registered
- LogBook.com — premium, registered
- ServiceTitan — $9.5B FSM platform (not a direct comp but validates market)
- FSM-related .com domains trade at $1K–$5K for brandables

**Brandability:** 36/50 Tier 2 — Descriptive, clear industry use case. Three words but "field logbook" reads as a natural compound. Good for a SaaS product name or mobile app. Somewhat narrow but highly relevant to the right buyer.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $2,000
