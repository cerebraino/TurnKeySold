- **Primary Angle:** The Creator Brand. The AI content space is booming — newsletters, YouTube channels, podcasts, directories. "Top AI Guys" has the casual, community feel that works for content creators. It's not corporate, not academic — it's the brand for AI enthusiasts who want to discover and review the best tools.
- **Key Talking Points:** Casual, community-brand feel — perfect for content and creators. "Top AI Guys" has personality and memorability. Works for a review site, directory, podcast, or channel. The AI content space is growing fast — domain matches the energy.
