- **Angle for Duolingo:** "You teach languages to the world. infancia.ai is where children begin their learning journey. It's the childhood brand for the world's most successful language app."

- **Angle for Lingokids:** "You're already the leader in children's bilingual learning. infancia.ai completes your brand architecture — the .ai extension signals your AI-powered future."
- **Open:** $22,000 | **Floor:** $8,000
- **Cultural note:** "Infancia" is a deeply emotive word in Spanish. Pitch should emphasize the *mission* of early childhood education, not just commercial value.
- **SEO angle:** "Infancia" is a high-volume Spanish keyword (millions of monthly searches from parents). The domain has built-in organic traffic for Spanish-language edtech.
