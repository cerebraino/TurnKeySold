## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Mexican Cannabis" — formal Spanish term for Mexican cannabis. More professional and regulatory-friendly than slang alternatives. Positions for industry associations, B2B cannabis trade, regulatory compliance platforms, or professional cannabis media covering the Mexican and Latin American markets. Mexico legalized medical cannabis and is progressing toward full adult-use legalization — this domain anticipates that market.

**Comparables:**
- CannabisMexico.com — similar concept
- LatinoCannabis.com — comparable
- MexicoCannabis.org — trade association style
- Industry .com domains in cannabis trade at $2K–$8K

**Brandability:** 38/50 Tier 2 — Professional, descriptive, clear. "Mexi-" prefix + "cannabico" is formal and trustworthy. Better for B2B than consumer brand. 13 letters — a bit long but descriptive advantage.

**Value:** $500–$4,000 | **Anchor:** $3,000
