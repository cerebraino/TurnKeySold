## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Aspirational Brand. "Cerebrado" isn't just "smart" — it's "brilliant." It's what students aspire to be, what parents want for their children, and what professionals strive to become. As a domain, it works for any platform that helps people become smarter — language learning, professional skills, brain training, or AI-powered tutoring.
- **Key Talking Points:** Aspirational adjective — positive, warm, motivational. Works across edtech, brain training, cognitive wellness, and AI learning. .com is essential for education trust in Spanish-speaking markets. Memorable single word — easy to share and recall.
- **For Duolingo specifically:** Luis von Ahn is Guatemalan and deeply connected to Spanish-language education. "Cerebrado" is the Spanish-language companion to the "brainy" owl brand.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone (Spanish):** Warm, encouraging, aspirational. Education is an emotional category — the domain should feel like a compliment and an invitation.
- **Tone (English):** Market expansion. Spanish-language edtech is a massive growth sector; frame as the trust domain for entering it.
- **Key insight:** "Cerebrado" is an adjective you'd use to describe a brilliant person. As a brand, it flatters the user before they've even started learning. That positive association is the domain's secret weapon.
