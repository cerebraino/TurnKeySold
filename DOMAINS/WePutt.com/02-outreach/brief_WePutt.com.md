# Campaign Brief: WePutt.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** WePutt.com — "We Putt" community golf brand
- **Niche:** Golf / Putting / Golf Tech / Training Aids
- **Target Market:** Putting training aids, golf tech apps, putting green chains, PGA Tour leagues
- **Value Prop:** "We Putt" = community, belonging, and the most important part of golf. "Drive for show, putt for dough." This domain owns the putting community — everyone from weekend golfers to PGA pros.

- **Primary Angle:** The Community of Putting. "We Putt" is inclusive, social, and aspirational. It says: putting is what we do together. Perfect for a putting community, training platform, or entertainment brand.
- **Key Talking Points:**
  - Putting is 40%+ of strokes in golf — and the most neglected part of the game.
  - "We Putt" speaks to every golfer. It's community-first.
  - Short (6 letters + .com), clean, immediately understood.
  - The putting training aid market is $500M+ and growing.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Social, inclusive, passionate. Golfers love putting — speak their language. "Drive for show, putt for dough" is the universal golf truth.
- **Golf industry insight:** The putting market is under-served digitally. Most brands are physical products (mats, training aids) — owning the community domain is a first-mover advantage.
- **Entertainment angle:** TopGolf proved putting is entertainment. "We Putt" could be the brand for the next putting experience.

## Objection Handling
- **"We're a product company, not a community":** "Every product company in golf is becoming a community. 18Birdies built a community around scoring. PuttOut has a passionate following. WePutt.com is the domain that turns your customers into a community — and communities buy more products."
- **"Putting is niche — we need broader golf positioning":** "Putting accounts for 40%+ of strokes. Every golfer thinks about putting more than any other part of their game. 'We Putt' is specific enough to own the category and broad enough to encompass everything putting-related — training, entertainment, tech, and community."
- **"The price is high for a .com":** "Premium golf domains are scarce. Comparable domains in the golf space have sold for $10K–$25K. WePutt.com is a one-time investment in a community brand that could anchor your digital presence."
- **"We already have a brand name":** "Great brands acquire community domains all the time. WePutt.com could be your putting-focused sub-brand, content platform, or community hub — not a replacement, but an expansion."
