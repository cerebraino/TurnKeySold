## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Field Work Order" — enterprise field service work order management. More specific than fieldlogbook.com — this is about work order creation, dispatch, and tracking for field crews. Targets the same FSM industry but with a narrower, higher-intent keyword. "Work order" is a core FSM function with high search volume.

**Comparables:**
- WorkOrder.com — premium, registered
- FieldWorkOrder.com — this domain itself
- ServiceTitan, ServiceMax — major FSM platforms that include work order modules
- "Work order" is a high-value keyword in field service SaaS

**Brandability:** 35/50 Tier 2 — Highly descriptive, clear B2B function. Three words but "field work order" is a standard industry phrase. Less brandable as a company name, but excellent for a product module, feature page, or SEO landing page.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $2,000
