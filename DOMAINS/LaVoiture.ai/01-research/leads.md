## 5. Outreach Recommendations

### Best Personas to Target
1. **Persona A (Visionary/CEO):** Stellantis, Renault, Bugatti — pitch as a flagship AI sub-brand or innovation portal
2. **Persona B (Strategist/Founder):** Verkor, ACC, Mistral AI — pitch as the definitive brand for their category

### Suggested Angles (from campaign brief)
- **Primary:** "La Voiture.ai is to French automotive AI what Château Margaux is to French wine — definitive, prestigious, unmistakable."
- **For Startups:** "Establish category authority immediately. LaVoiture.ai tells investors and customers you're THE player in automotive AI."
- **For Industrialists:** "Bridge your heritage with AI innovation. Own the definitive French automotive domain for your next-gen brand."

### Talking Points for Outreach Architect
- **Cultural note:** For French buyers, emphasize *prestige* and *patrimoine* (heritage) meeting *innovation* — as outlined in the campaign brief.
- **Urgency angle:** .ai extension prices are rising; French premium domains with definitive articles are scarce.
- **Price anchoring:** Open at $42,000; floor at $18,000 for serious buyers.
- **Competitive threat angle:** "Would you rather own this, or see a competitor acquire and redirect it?"
