## Market Context

"Quitar" in Portuguese means "to pay off / settle / liquidate" — specifically a debt. "Quite sua casa" is the phrase Brazilians search when they want to become debt-free homeowners. It's the dream: quitar o financiamento, ser dono de verdade.

Brazil has one of the world's largest mortgage markets (R$800B+ in outstanding home loans). Home equity lending is exploding as a fintech category. And "Minha Casa Minha Vida" — Brazil's flagship housing program — has millions of beneficiaries actively paying down government-subsidized mortgages.
