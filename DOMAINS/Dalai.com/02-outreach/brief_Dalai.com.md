# Campaign Brief: Dalai.com (Anchor) + Full Package

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** Dalai.com — Premium 5-letter .com with wellness/tech positioning
- **Niche:** Wellness/Tech / Meditation / Coined Brand
- **Value Prop:** "Dalai" is a rhythmic 5-letter brand name — calming, memorable, premium .com. Positioned as an *independent coined word* for wellness/tech, NOT referencing any spiritual figure.

> ⚠️ **TRADEMARK DISCLOSURE:** "Dalai" is associated with the Dalai Lama, a protected name. Buyers must consult IP counsel and demonstrate independent, non-confusing use (e.g., wellness/tech brand with no spiritual/religious association). This domain's value derives from "Dalai" as a coined, rhythmic word — not from any association with a public figure. **This risk must be disclosed to all buyers.**

## Trademark Objection Handler (CRITICAL — must be ready for every conversation)
- **"Dalai is too closely tied to the Dalai Lama — we can't use it":** "That's a fair concern, and it's why we're being upfront about it. Many successful brands have names that overlap with known terms — Apple, Amazon, Delta, Oracle. The key is independent, non-confusing use. 'Dalai' as a wellness/tech brand has no religious or spiritual context. That said, we recommend every buyer consult IP counsel. We're pricing the domain accordingly — the risk is reflected in the price."
- **"The trademark risk makes it too expensive":** "We agree the risk affects value. That's why we're open to flexible pricing and terms. The bundle — Dalai.com + Dalai.co + Dalai.fit — gives you full brand coverage at a price that reflects the trademark consideration."
- **"We'd rather pick a completely neutral name":** "Respect that. The advantage of 'Dalai' is it's already known — 5 letters, rhythmic, sticks in memory. Starting from scratch with a completely unknown word costs millions in brand building. With proper positioning, Dalai can be your coined word."
