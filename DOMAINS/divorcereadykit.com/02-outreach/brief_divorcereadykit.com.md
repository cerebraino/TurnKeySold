## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Search-Intent Domain. "Divorce Ready Kit" is exactly what someone types when they've decided to move forward and need the tools. It's a high-intent, high-conversion search phrase in a category where customer acquisition costs are significant. For any online divorce platform, this domain captures ready-to-buy traffic.
- **Key Talking Points:** Exact-match for "divorce ready kit" searches — high commercial intent. DIY/online divorce market is growing rapidly as courts modernize. Domain can serve as a standalone product page, lead-generation funnel, or redirect to existing divorce services. "Ready Kit" implies a complete, packaged solution — premium positioning.
- **Product angle:** Unlike abstract brand names, this domain describes the product itself — a kit that gets you divorce-ready. It pre-sells the offering before the visitor even clicks.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Professional, empathetic, solution-oriented. Divorce is a sensitive topic — the domain is practical, not emotional. Keep messaging focused on the business value (customer acquisition, search intent capture) rather than the emotional aspects of divorce.
- **Key insight:** The word "Ready" is powerful in this context. It signals that the person has moved past indecision and is in action mode. The domain meets them at that exact moment.
- **Category note:** This domain works best for platforms that offer downloadable/guided divorce products — kits, document packages, step-by-step guides. It's less suited for full-service law firms.
