# Campaign Brief: paydeal.ai

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** paydeal.ai
- **Niche:** BNPL / Deal Platforms / Promotional Payments / Buy Now Pay Later
- **Target Market:** Global — BNPL providers, deal marketplaces, installment payment platforms
- **Value Prop:** "Pay Deal" — the domain that captures the entire BNPL economy. Deals + payments + AI = the future of how people buy.

## Target Buyer Personas
1. **The BNPL Leader (Persona A/B):** CEOs of leading BNPL platforms (Affirm, Klarna, Afterpay) where "pay for a deal on your terms" is the core offering.
2. **The Deals Marketplace (Persona B):** Deal/coupon platforms (Groupon, Rakuten) looking for an AI-powered pivot and brand refresh.
3. **The Embedded BNPL Startup (Persona B):** Growing BNPL providers (Zip, Sezzle) looking for a brand upgrade from generic names.

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Deal Economy. BNPL is transforming how people pay — "paydeal.ai" captures both sides: the deal (what you buy) and the payment (how you pay). AI-powered deal discovery + flexible payments.
- **Key Talking Points:**
  - "Pay" + "Deal" = the entire BNPL value proposition in two words.
  - Short (7 chars + .ai), clean, action-oriented.
  - The .ai signals AI-powered deal recommendations and payment optimization.
