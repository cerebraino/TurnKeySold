## 3. Domain Positioning Strategy

### "Puttey" as a Brand
- **Best as:** A golf app name, putting practice companion, putting analytics brand, or putting coaching brand
- **Not ideal for:** Hardware/equipment (sounds too playful), luxury brands (sounds too casual)
- **Consumer appeal:** Friendly, approachable, fun — perfect for amateur golfers who love their putter

### vs. WePutt.com
| Factor | WePutt.com | Puttey.com |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Positioning | Community/league/inclusive | Personal/friendly/app-like |
| Best use | Golf community, league, media | Golf app, training aid brand, putting coach |
| Value | $5K–$20K | $3K–$12K |
| Top buyer | PuttOut (community extension) | PuttView (consumer app brand) |
