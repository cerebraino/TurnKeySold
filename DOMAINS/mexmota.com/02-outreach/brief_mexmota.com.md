## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Culturally authentic, not appropriative. "Mota" is a real term with real cultural weight — use it respectfully.
- **Key insight:** "Mota" carries more cultural authenticity than "cannabis" or "marijuana" for Mexican consumers. It's the word people actually use — not the word regulators prefer.
