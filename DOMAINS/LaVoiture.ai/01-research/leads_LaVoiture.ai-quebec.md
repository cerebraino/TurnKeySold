## 1. Why Quebec for "La Voiture"?

"La Voiture" is **natural Quebecois French.** Unlike France, where "une voiture" is a generic car, in Quebec, "La Voiture" carries a distinctive cultural identity — the French-Canadian language is a core part of Quebec's identity. The .ai extension signals AI innovation in a province that houses **Mila — one of the world's top 3 AI research institutes** — alongside a growing auto/AI ecosystem.
