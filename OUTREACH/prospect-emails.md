> For enterprise leaders, firstname.lastname@company.com is the standard pattern.

## Tips for LinkedIn

Since you have a LinkedIn subscription (Sales Navigator?), you can:
1. Search for each person by name + company
2. Send a connection request with a brief note
3. Once connected, LinkedIn may show their email
4. Or use the LinkedIn InMail system to message them directly

## If You Want Verified Emails

For verified (guaranteed correct) emails, you can use:
- **Apollo.io** — free tier gives 50 verified emails/month
- **Hunter.io** — 25 free verifications/month
- **Skrapp.io** — free email finder
- **Prospect.io** — LinkedIn + email combo

These tools verify the email against the company's mail server before returning it, so you never send to a dead address.
