# Campaign Brief: jogpay.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** jogpay.com
- **Niche:** Fitness Fintech / Activity-Based Rewards / Health Payments
- **Target Market:** Fitness apps, activity tracking platforms, wellness reward programs
- **Value Prop:** "Jog Pay" — the domain for fitness-linked payments and rewards. As the intersection of fitness tracking and fintech grows (earn rewards for steps, get paid to exercise, activity-based insurance discounts), this domain captures the concept in two short words. Six characters, action-oriented, and positioned at a growing niche.

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Fitness Rewards Brand. "Jog Pay" captures the concept of getting paid or rewarded for physical activity. As fitness apps increasingly integrate financial incentives — from Sweatcoin's step rewards to StepBet's cash stakes — this domain is the brand that bridges jogging and payments.
- **Key Talking Points:** 6 characters — short, punchy, memorable. Two everyday words combined into a clear concept. Fitness + fintech is a growing crossover category. Action-oriented — "jog" is the activity, "pay" is the reward.
- **Ideal for:** A fitness rewards app, activity-based insurance product, or wellness incentive platform.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Energetic, reward-focused, fitness-native. "Jog" is accessible — not just for marathoners.
- **Key insight:** The concept of "getting paid to exercise" has proven consumer appeal. Sweatcoin has millions of users. This domain captures that concept in a brandable format.

## Objection Handling
- **"'Jog' is too narrow — we cover all fitness":** "'Jog' is the most accessible fitness verb — it's shorthand for any cardio activity. Like 'Kickstarter' isn't just for kicking, 'Jog Pay' isn't just for jogging. It's a memorable brand, not a literal description."
