## 5. Outreach Recommendations

### Best Personas to Target
1. **Persona B (Founder/CEO) — #1 Priority:** Ro, Hims & Hers, Capsule — these are founder-led companies with brand authority tied to the CEO
2. **Persona A (Visionary/Enterprise):** Noom, WeightWatchers — public/mature companies where CEO-driven framing works well
3. **Persona E (Agency expansion):** PillPack/Amazon Pharmacy, CVS — longer sales cycle but highest ASP

### Suggested Angles (from campaign brief)
- **Primary:** "LeanMeds.com is the name that tells the market you're the efficient, modern alternative in health-tech. It's Hims meets clinical authority."
- **For DTC founders:** "Your CAC drops the moment your domain communicates what you do. LeanMeds.com does the selling for you."
- **For pharmacy platforms:** "A direct-to-consumer brand that costs less to acquire customers because the name itself is the value prop."

### Talking Points for Outreach Architect
- **Hot leads:** Ro, Hims & Hers — these should get highest priority. Founder-CEOs who have publicly stated brand matters.
- **Urgency angle:** GLP-1 market is the gold rush of 2024–2026; brands that establish themselves now dominate the category.
- **Price anchoring:** Open at $25,000; floor at $12,000.
- **Competitive angle for Ro/Hims:** "Your competitor already has a .com that matches their offering. Here's yours."
- **Tone (from brief):** Efficient, trustworthy, solution-oriented. Emphasize CAC reduction and brand recall.
