# Campaign Brief: bajaturenta.com

## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** bajaturenta.com
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Rental Savings / Rent Negotiation / Tenant Services
- **Target Market:** US Hispanic renters, Latin American rental markets, real estate platforms, rent negotiation services
- **Value Prop:** "Baja Tu Renta" = "Lower Your Rent" in Spanish. A direct, action-oriented domain addressing one of the top financial concerns for Spanish-speaking renters. For a rental platform, rent negotiation service, or tenant advocacy tool, this domain is the promise that converts: "we'll lower your rent."

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Rent Relief Promise. "Baja Tu Renta" is what every renter wants to hear. For a rental platform expanding into Hispanic markets or a rent negotiation service, this domain is the marketing promise that immediately resonates. It's a command, a benefit, and a brand all at once.
- **Key Talking Points:** "Baja tu renta" is a high-intent Spanish search phrase. Rental affordability is a top concern for Hispanic renters. Three words that make a direct promise — no explanation needed. .com for trust in financial/rental contexts. Clean, action-oriented, memorable.
- **Use case:** Spanish-language rental landing page, rent negotiation service brand, or Hispanic renter outreach campaign.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone (Spanish):** Direct, benefit-focused, empathetic. Rent is a pain point — the domain acknowledges that and offers relief.
- **Key insight:** "Baja tu renta" is an imperative — it tells the user what will happen. Command-form domains convert at higher rates because they promise action, not just information.

## Objection Handling
- **"We're a listing platform, not a negotiation service":** "bajaturenta.com can be a marketing campaign or landing page — 'find apartments and lower your rent.' It's a traffic-driving promise, not necessarily a product description. The domain captures search intent and funnels it to your platform."
