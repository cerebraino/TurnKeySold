Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a campaign take?
The outreach phase runs 3–4 weeks. Premium domain sales can close anywhere from 2 weeks to 6 months depending on buyer readiness. We stay on it until it sells.
Do I need to transfer my domain to you?
No. You retain full ownership in your registrar account. You grant us authorization to market it — nothing more.
What if my domain doesn't sell?
You pay nothing. The success fee applies only to closed and funded sales.
How do I know I'll actually get paid?
All transactions run through licensed escrow (e.g., Escrow.com). Funds are verified and secured before any domain transfer takes place. We never touch your funds.
Can I sell the domain myself during the campaign?
Yes. You're free to pursue your own sales channels. If you find a buyer independently, simply let us know.
What types of domains do you work with?
Premium .com, .ai, .io, and select country TLDs typically valued at $5,000+. We review each submission individually.
