## Core Insight

"Mini terapia" = mini/short/brief therapy. In Spanish-speaking markets, **"terapia breve"** (brief therapy) is an established therapeutic modality with deep academic and clinical roots. Solution-focused therapy, strategic therapy (MRI model, Giorgio Nardone), and single-session therapy are widely practiced in Spain, Mexico, Argentina, and across LATAM. The domain miniterapia.com perfectly captures the accessibility and time-limited format that this entire therapeutic tradition represents.
