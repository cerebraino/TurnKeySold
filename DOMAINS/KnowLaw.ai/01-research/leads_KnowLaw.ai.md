### Talking Points for Outreach Architect
- **Hot lead — Harvey AI:** Contact immediately. Most likely to understand immediate value. Suggested open: $38,000.
- **Tone (from brief):** Intelligent, authoritative, forward-thinking. Legal-tech buyers value precision.
- **Price anchoring:** Open at $38,000; floor at $15,000.
