## Domain Overview
- **Domain:** miniterapia.com
- **Niche:** Spanish-Language Mental Health / Micro-Therapy / Digital Wellness

## Messaging Strategy
- **Primary Angle:** The Accessible Therapy Brand. "Mini" signals short, accessible, bite-sized — exactly the direction mental health is heading. Combined with "Terapia," it's warm and inviting rather than clinical and intimidating. Perfect for an app, a service tier, or a Spanish-language mental wellness portal.
- **Key Talking Points:** "Mini" removes the intimidation barrier — therapy doesn't have to be a big commitment. "Terapia" is the trusted Spanish word for therapy. Perfect for apps offering short sessions, daily check-ins, or AI-powered support. 11 characters — clean and memorable domain.

## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone (Spanish):** Warm, approachable, destigmatizing. Mental health carries cultural stigma in many Hispanic communities — "Mini Terapia" feels light and accessible, not heavy and clinical.
- **Key insight:** The word "Mini" is strategic. It signals that therapy can be small, manageable, and fit into daily life — countering the perception that therapy is a major, intimidating commitment.

## Objection Handling
- **"We already offer therapy in Spanish":** "miniterapia.com captures a different user — someone looking for quick, accessible support in Spanish. It can be a dedicated landing page, a product tier ('Mini Terapia' sessions), or a Spanish-language funnel."
- **"'Mini' — does it trivialize therapy?":** "Quite the opposite. 'Mini' makes it approachable. For someone hesitant about therapy, 'Mini Terapia' feels like a safe first step — not a major commitment. That's the conversion advantage."
- **"Is the Spanish-language mental health market sizable?":** "500M+ Spanish speakers globally, with mental health awareness growing rapidly in LATAM and US Hispanic communities. The market for accessible, Spanish-language mental health tools is expanding fast."
