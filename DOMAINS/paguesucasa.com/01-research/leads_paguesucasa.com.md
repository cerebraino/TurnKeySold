## 1. Domain Valuation

**Concept:** "Pay Your House" — Spanish mortgage/real estate payments domain. Targets mortgage lenders, real estate fintech platforms, home loan comparison sites, and mortgage payment processing companies serving the Spanish-speaking market. "Pague su casa" is a formal command form — professional tone suitable for financial services. The mortgage market for US Hispanic and Latin American homebuyers is massive and growing.

**Comparables:**
- PayYourHouse.com — English equivalent
- QuickenLoans.com — Rocket Mortgage's former domain
- Better.com — $7B digital mortgage platform
- Spanish mortgage domains trade at $500–$3K

**Brandability:** 34/50 Tier 2 — Clear action, financial service tone. Three words but "pague su casa" is a natural mortgage phrase. Better for a service/product than a company name. Good SEO potential for Spanish mortgage searches.

**Value:** $500–$3,000 | **Anchor:** $1,500
