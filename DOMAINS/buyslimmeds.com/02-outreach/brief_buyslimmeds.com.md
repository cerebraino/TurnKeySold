## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Direct, ROI-focused, commercially savvy. These are healthcare CEOs running multi-billion dollar companies. Speak in terms of customer acquisition cost, search volume, and brand efficiency.
- **Avoid:** Over-promising about the medical benefits of weight loss medications. Stay focused on the commercial value of the domain.
- **Sensitive note:** The weight loss industry has a history of body shaming and unhealthy practices. Position the domain as a tool for *health access* (making it easier for people to find legitimate medical weight loss care) vs. promoting thinness.
- **Bundle strategy:** Ro and Hims are the top leads for both buyslimmeds.com AND LeanMeds.com. When pitching to them, mention both domains as a complementary pair — one professional (LeanMeds) and one transactional (buyslimmeds).
