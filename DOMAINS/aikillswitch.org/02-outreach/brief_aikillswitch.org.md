## Tone & Cultural Considerations
- **Tone:** Serious, urgent, mission-aligned. AI safety is not a light topic — the domain and messaging should match the gravity of the subject.
- **Key insight:** The term "kill switch" carries inherent urgency and controversy. For an AI safety organization, that's a feature — it signals that you're engaged with the hard questions, not avoiding them.
- **.org credibility:** The .org extension is essential here. A .com "kill switch" domain could feel commercial or alarmist. .org signals research, advocacy, and public interest.
